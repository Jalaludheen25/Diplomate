Redefining Business Freedom

UAE Free Zones are designated economic areas where entrepreneurs can enjoy a highly conducive environment for business growth. Since the first free zone was established, these hubs have become the cornerstone of Dubai's status as a global commercial powerhouse.

At Diplomate, we provide a curated selection of over 45 free zones, each tailored to specific industries—from technology and media to trading and logistics.

Core Strategic Advantages

  • 01. 100% Foreign Ownership: Maintain total control of your enterprise without the requirement of a local partner.
  • 02. Tax Optimization: Benefit from a 0% corporate and personal income tax environment, ensuring maximum ROI.
  • 03. Capital Repatriation: Enjoy the freedom to repatriate 100% of your capital and profits to your home country.
