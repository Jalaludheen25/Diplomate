Overview

Why choose our Ejari Registration service?

Navigating the administrative landscape in Dubai requires precision. Our dedicated experts handle every technicality of your Ejari Registration, ensuring 100% compliance with local laws, incredibly fast turnaround times, and total peace of mind.

Requirements & Process

Everything you need to know and the steps we take to ensure your success.

Key Requirements

  • Original documents to be processed/attested
  • Passport and Visa copies of involved parties
  • Company Trade License
  • Authorization letter / Power of Attorney

How It Works

1

Document Collection

Secure pickup of your original documents from your location.

2

Translation & Typing

Legally translating and formatting documents for government acceptance.

3

Authority Submission

Processing through Notary Public, MOFA, or respective Embassies.

4

Secure Delivery

Safe return of your attested, legally stamped documents directly to you.
